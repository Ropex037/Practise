Schadule a meeting

1.We personally know good engineers

All the candidates are our friends and acquaintances who we've met through the years at lectures, conferences, competitions and other events. We view personally knowing the candidates as a huge advantage, and it creates a big difference between us and other recruiting and headhunting agencies. We recognize all the interests, preferences and experience of our candidates and that gives us the ability to propose to them the best projects for their career, which benefits them as well as the company in which they will be employed. The candidates we know are from various profiles and experience levels and we're always open to meeting new people who would like our help in finding the best company for them. The candidates will never have to pay for our services.

2. We understand our clients

We ourselves have years of experience working in this industry and understand the needs our clients have for their projects, as well as the knowledge, experience and traits the ideal candidate should have for the job.

Our conditions are simple and clear which benefits our clients, as well as the candidates we recommend. Our clients pay only after they've decided that one of our candidates fulfills all their desired skills and characteristics and after they employ our candidate.

We give all our clients assurance in case they later part ways with any candidate who was recommended by us.

3.We always recommend the best

After meeting with the client, we contact the candidates who we think would be a good fit for the chosen position and then recommend and connect the candidates with the client.


The next step is for the client to decide if the candidate matches their expectations, but we try to make the process easier by recommending only the candidates who we truly believe would make the cut. Our main focus is to find only a small number of clients who have the needed skills and experience.



Assurance

We are giving assurance to our clients, no matter which job position or candidate experience they want.

Pay after successful employmentu

Our clients pay for our services only if they hire a candidate we had previously recommended. If none of our suggestions satisfies the client's needs, our prior services remain completely free.

Pro-rata payment

After hiring one of our candidates, you can pay the expenses for our services in equal cuts (9 month period), up until the candidate is still employed at your company. This assurance is ideal for companies which have a trial period.

One year guarantee

If the candidate leaves the company within the first year of employment, his replacement, our next recommendation will be for free..
