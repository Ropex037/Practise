FAQ

Are mover more expensive during the weekends?

Since weekend moves are more popular and convenient for most people, some companies do charge more for weekend moves. Give you moveing company a call and ask if there is any diffrences in pricing on weekends ad opposed to weekday moves, and if there is any possibility of saveing money by moveing your date around, that is great option to have.

Will movers pack your items?

Most professional companies offer the service of both packing your items before moveing them. Even though this service dose come with some additional expenses, having your moving company pack up everything you own can save you a lot of time and effort in the long run. Your moving company will also make an inventory of your behalf, and even help you unpack, so this is a convenient option to have when looking for professional movers to handle your relocation.

How much a movers charge per hour?

Hom much will be charge for your move can depend on quite a large number of aspect, such as the difrence between you two location, the numbers of movers need to preform your move, and if you are looking for any additional moving services. Requesting a quote from moving companies is the only way to find out exectly how much you will be charge per hour and your moving company can offer provide you with an estimate how much you will end up paying.
