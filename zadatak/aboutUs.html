About Us

We want to show you exactly how amazing your moving experience can be, and how easily we can get the job done.

The moving adventure of Plymouth Meeting Movers began in early 2008, in Plymouth Meeting, PA. A locally-owned business, we have truly built our business from square one. This has not only humbled us, and shaped us into the business we are today, but it has also made us fall in love with our community even more. We have assisted countless families and single residents of Plymouth Meeting since the day we have opened our moving doors, and we have continued to grow over the years and developed some of the best moving methods in the entire community.

There is a simple approach that leads to success – and that is listening to our clients, assessing their needs meticulously, and providing them with a straightforward moving service that will fulfill their wishes and deliver an experience that they will be content with. We know that there are a lot of unlicensed, uninsured movers out there, that don’t care about your relocation but see you as a number, but we always strive to take the approach of welcoming our clients with open arms and making them comfortable with their entire experience with Plymouth Meeting Movers. This is exactly how we have earned the reputation of one of the best, most reputable furniture movers and packers in Plymouth Meeting, and why our community loves us.

We want to provide you with a moving experience that you will be fully content with, as we understand that your relocation is not just moving your stuff from point A to point B. Your relocation is stressful, emotional, and daunting, and we wish to contribute to lessening these negative feelings as much as possible.

We want to deliver a service that you will be completely content with, and we are a team of movers that loves getting things done properly or not at all.

We are a local moving company that constantly invests in training their moves, purchasing only the best industry preferred equipment, the best, modern moving vehicles and the needed moving resources to provide you with an impeccable moving service that never lacks in quality.

We also offer each and every single service at extremely competitive rates, that will ultimately result in a flawless service that doesn’t cost an arm and a leg. We have a wide range of moving services to choose from, which we will also gladly tailor to your wishes and provide you with a unique moving experience that you will be satisfied with.

We want to hear from you, hear about your moving requirements and thoughts, and implement them in our moving techniques. Your satisfaction is always our top priority, and we will treat the entire process of your relocation with utmost care and respect. We know how stressful moving can be – which is why we will go above and beyond in making it everything but that. We are confident that we have exactly what it takes to provide you with the perfect relocation, which is why you should reach out to us today.
